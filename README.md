# Archive - Joint Committee for the Scrutiny of Regulations

Script downloads and archives the Notices of Meeting, the Evidence, and the Minutes of each meeting of the Joint Committee for the Scrutiny of Regulations (REGS Committee).

The [output](http://regs.policygeek.ca/output.csv) is 13Mb of uncompressed data with the Notice of Meeting, Evidence, and Minutes of each meeting of the REGS Committee since the 38th parliament before which they did not have Notices of Meeting.
